Process

From photo to plate, in four steps.

The same pipeline runs whether you're checking one vehicle or scanning a whole fleet.

01 / UPLOAD

Upload or capture

Drag in a photo, or snap one straight from your phone's camera — rear camera opens automatically.

02 / DETECT

Locate the plate

The model finds the plate region in your image, even at an angle or in low light.

03 / EXTRACT

Read the number

OCR pulls the plate text and formats it to standard Indian plate convention.

04 / LOOKUP

Get vehicle info

Make, model, colour and RTO details appear alongside a confidence score.

Capabilities

Built for daily, real-world use.

Every feature is designed around how vehicles actually get scanned — outdoors, on the move, on a phone.

Detection & OCR extraction

Locates the plate and reads it in one pass, formatted to standard convention automatically.

Vehicle info lookup

Make, model, colour, fuel type and registering RTO, matched against the extracted plate.

History & reports

Every scan is saved to your account with a downloadable PDF report you can share or file.

Batch processing

Fleet and business accounts can queue multiple images and process them together.

Accurate in low light

Preprocessing corrects for angle, glare and dim conditions before the text is read.

Manual correction

If confidence is low, you can edit and confirm the reading in one tap before saving it.

Since 1996

Built in Bardoli, for real, everyday recognition.

VNPR started under Kritesh Yadav as a way to solve a very local problem: keeping track of which vehicles come and go, quickly and accurately, without a paper register.

Nearly three decades later, that same practical focus carries into the AI behind VNPR — built to work reliably outdoors, on ordinary phone cameras, for the shopkeepers, lot operators and fleet managers who rely on it every day.
